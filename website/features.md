## 🌟 Feature Highlights

### Playlist Management
- Create playlists for any mood or occasion.
- Edit and reorder songs with simple drag-and-drop functionality.
- Delete playlists when no longer needed.

### Song and Album Library
- Save songs and albums for quick and easy access.
- Discover your favourite artist’s full discography with one click.

### Powerful Tagging System
- Add and manage custom tags to categorize songs.
- Filter songs and playlists quickly using tags like "Chill," "Workout," or "Favourite."

### Advanced Sorting Options
- Sort songs by metrics such as:
    - **Duration:** Perfect for planning setlists or workout sessions.
    - **Artist:** Quickly group songs by the same creator.
    - **Title (Alphabetical):** Easily find what you’re looking for.

### Seamless Search
- Search by song title or album name to find what you need instantly.

### Comprehensive Song Details
- View detailed information about each song, including:
    - **Runtime:** Know how long the track lasts.
    - **Artist and Album:** See where it comes from.
    - **Associated Tags:** Organize your library at a glance.
